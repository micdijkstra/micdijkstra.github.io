About

My approach is simple: new ideas are born on paper with a thick black sharpie and then designed in code and deployed regularly. Every new feature I build has the job-to-be-done in mind. I believe a useful interface is more important than a beautiful interface and copywriting is design and should be included from the beginning.

I work best with small teams and strive to delight customers by building products that are simple and enjoyable to use.

Storyberg was my most recent startup and Wevther is my current side project, a collaboration with Jacob and Jack.

I'm currently working as Head of Product for PwC Australia's Create Digital practice.
